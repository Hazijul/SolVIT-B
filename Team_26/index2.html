Our Mission

In the realm of public service management, handling user feedback and queries efficiently is a critical challenge. Our project aims to enhance public service administration by leveraging AI-driven techniques to process and categorize feedback from users interacting with our train management system.

Our Vision

The system performs four key operations:

  • Detecting and filtering spam to ensure data relevance.
  • Classifying queries based on service domains (e.g., ticketing, scheduling).
  • Analyzing user feedback to predict satisfaction levels.
  • Assigning priority to queries for optimized resolution.
By automating these tasks, our model significantly improves the efficiency and effectiveness of public service response mechanisms.
